Our Story

Connecting the World Through Exceptional Craft

LuxeThread was founded on a simple belief: the finest garments deserve to reach the hands of those who appreciate them, no matter where in the world they are.

Our Mission

Curating the World's Best

We travel to the far corners of the globe — from the linen mills of Northern Italy to the silk weavers of Kyoto — to find garments that represent the pinnacle of their craft.

Every piece in our collection has been hand-selected by our team, who spend months each year building relationships with artisans and ensuring ethical, sustainable production practices.
